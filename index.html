Affective Sensory Evaluation – Paayasam Mix

Sensory Science · 9-Point Hedonic Scale
Behind the Moment

Served as a sensory panellist at Pavizham, participating in an affective sensory evaluation to identify the most consumer-preferred paayasam mix. The study used the 9-point hedonic scale — a gold-standard tool in sensory science that measures consumer liking and preference with reliable, reproducible results across different panels and product types.

This hands-on experience reinforced the importance of merging scientific rigour with culinary heritage. Evaluating culturally rooted products through structured sensory methodology highlighted how straightforward tools can generate powerful insights to guide product development decisions that truly resonate with consumers.

Food Waste Bioelectrochemical System

KSCSTE-CWRDM · Kozhikode, India

01

Sustainable Waste Management Dissertation

Bioelectrochemistry · Waste-to-Energy
Behind the Moment

For my dissertation, I focused on one of the most pressing environmental issues: food waste management. My research involved developing a food waste-based bioelectrochemical system using Microbial Fuel Cells (MFCs) to convert food waste into energy—a sustainable approach for household waste.

I constructed MFCs that utilized food waste as a substrate, harnessing microbial activity to generate electricity. The study included voltage measurements, MLVSS analysis, COD analysis, and polarization testing to evaluate energy potential. I am deeply grateful to KSCSTE-CWRDM and especially Er. Ihjas K., Scientist B, for their invaluable guidance throughout this journey.

02

Identifying Water Stress Hot Spots

Field Survey · Ecology & Environment
Behind the Moment

Joined the CWRDM team on a two-day field survey for the project "Identification of Water Stress Hot Spots in Kerala." Alongside a project fellow from the Department of Ecology and Environment, we visited Malappuram District, covering areas like Nilambur Municipality and Karulai Grama Panchayat.

Our work involved visiting municipal offices to gather official insights, conducting on-site visits to hotspot locations, surveying households to understand local challenges firsthand, and recording geographic coordinates for each location. This fieldwork was an invaluable experience that enhanced my field data collection, analytical, and geographical data recording skills.
